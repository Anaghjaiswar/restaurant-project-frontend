If you don’t see any reservations available, please call 78798540859.

If you are running late, please call the restaurant at (248) 540-5900. We hold the table for a grace period of 15 minutes before offering the reservation to someone on our waitlist. All patio reservations are weather permitting.

About lazeezEats

lazeezEats is so much more than a bowl of noodle soup. It’s an obsession - the soul of a nation. A delicious healthy dish that is eaten for breakfast, lunch and dinner, constantly tailored to suit the individual.
Working at lazeezEats is much the same. It’s more than a job, it’s a career. We take the work as seriously as the lazeezEats and we love it. We offer complete training programmes, fantastic pay, bonus schemes, amazing staff parties, lots of Eats and incredible opportunities for growth and progression, in the kitchen and on the floor.
Whatever the role you are looking for, join the lazeezEats family and become part of our national obsession and we'll tailor your career to suit you.
